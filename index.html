Change Log

How to use

Same flow as your original tool, just cleaner and easier to maintain.

Typical workflow

1) Choose Account (Item #2).
2) Enter “Amount listed up top” (Item #3).
3) Enter Warehouse Protected for farms (Item #4).
4) Enter bag quantities (Item #5).
5) Repeat for next farm/main.

Notes

• Farms show lootable totals (protected amounts subtracted).
• Header row repeats in Item #5 every 8 rows for easier reading.

Reset options

Clear Current Account Inputs resets item #3–#5 inputs only.
Clear Saved wipes everything saved for this page on this device.
